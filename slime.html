SLIME!

The Basics

Ingredients:

  • glue
  • borax
  • water

OPTIONAL Ingredients:

  • scents
  • colouring
  • shaving cream
  • sequins/glitter

Steps:

Make Borax Solution:

  • Boil Water.
  • Pour a small amount of Borax into the water.
  • Stir.

Mix Glue and Solution:

  • Add a small amount of solution at a time.
  • If it gets too hard, add shaving cream.

Add-Ins!!

Add coloring, scents, or glitter!
